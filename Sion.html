Sion guide

This is a sion guide to help you learn and have more fun playing this champion

Tips and tricks

1. Use your q ability from bushes to hide them

2. Never group with your team if your r is on cooldown
3. Dont be scared of dying
4. Use your passive to set up waves to your advantage in laning phase
5. Sion is very good at proxying waves if you have a bad matchup
